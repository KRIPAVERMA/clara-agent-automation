## OFFICE HOURS CALL FLOW

1. **Greeting**: "Thank you for calling {company_name}. My name is Clara, how can I help you today?"
2. **Ask Purpose**: Determine the reason for the call.
3. **Collect Caller Information**:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
4. **Transfer Call**: Attempt to transfer the caller to the appropriate department or person.
5. **Fallback if Transfer Fails**: "I'm sorry, the line is currently busy. I've noted your information and someone will call you back shortly."
6. **Ask if Anything Else**: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
7. **Close Call**: "Thank you for calling {company_name}. Have a great day!"

---

## AFTER HOURS CALL FLOW

1. **Greeting**: "Thank you for calling {company_name}. Our office is currently closed. Our business hours are {business_hours}."
2. **Ask Purpose**: "Are you calling about an emergency or a general inquiry?"
3. **Confirm Emergency**: Determine if the situation qualifies as an emergency based on the emergency definition.

### If EMERGENCY:
4. **Collect Emergency Information**:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - Address / location of emergency
5. **Attempt Transfer**: Route the call per emergency routing rules.
6. **If Transfer Fails**: "I understand this is urgent. I've logged your information and our on-call team will be notified immediately. Someone will contact you as soon as possible."

### If NON-EMERGENCY:
4. **Collect Details**: Gather relevant information about the inquiry.
5. **Confirm Callback**: "I've noted your information. Someone from our team will call you back on the next business day."
6. **Close Call**: "Thank you for calling {company_name}. Have a good evening!"
